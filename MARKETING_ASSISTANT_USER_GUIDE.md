# AI Marketing Assistant - Quick Start Guide

## Overview
The AI Marketing Assistant is a new feature in Lake County Local Deals that helps vendors create professional marketing content for their deals across multiple channels using Claude AI.

## Accessing the System

1. **Log in** as a vendor to your dashboard
2. **Navigate** to `/vendor/marketing` or click "Marketing Assistant" in the vendor menu
3. You'll see the marketing dashboard with your active deals

## How It Works

### Step 1: Select a Content Type
Choose one of four content generation options:
- **üìß Email Campaigns** - Newsletter and promotional emails
- **üë• Social Media Posts** - Content for Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter
- **üì¢ Ad Copy** - Google Ads and Facebook Ads text
- **üè∑Ô∏è In-Store Signage** - Window clings, posters, and counter displays

### Step 2: Select a Deal
From your list of active deals, choose the one you want to create marketing content for. You'll see the deal title and current sale price.

### Step 3: Choose Platform (if applicable)
For some content types, select which platform you're creating for:
- **Social Media:** Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter/X
- **Ads:** Google Ads or Facebook Ads
- **Email & Signage:** No platform selection needed

### Step 4: Generate Content
Click the **"Generate Content"** button. The system will:
1. Send your deal information to Claude AI
2. Generate marketing content tailored to your deal and platform
3. Display the results (takes 10-15 seconds)

### Step 5: Review & Copy
Review the generated content and click **"Copy"** buttons to:
- Copy individual subject lines or sections
- Copy all hashtags at once
- Copy headlines and descriptions

### Step 6: Use Your Content
Paste the content into your marketing channels:
- **Email:** Mailchimp, Resend, Constant Contact, etc.
- **Social Media:** Facebook Business Suite, Instagram Creator Studio, Twitter Web, etc.
- **Ads:** Google Ads Manager, Facebook Ads Manager
- **Signage:** Print service or in-house printing

### Step 7: Mark as Used (Optional)
Click **"Mark as Used"** to track which content you've implemented. This helps you see your usage rates.

## Daily Limits

Your daily limit depends on your subscription tier:

| Subscription | Daily Limit | Renewal |
|--------------|------------|---------|
| Free | 5 content pieces | Daily at midnight |
| Starter | 10 content pieces | Daily at midnight |
| Basic | 25 content pieces | Daily at midnight |
| Pro | 100 content pieces | Daily at midnight |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | No limit |

**Note:** Each "content piece" is one generation (email, social post, ad copy, etc.). You can see your remaining content in the top right of the dashboard.

## Usage Statistics

The right sidebar shows you:
- **Daily Limit:** Your total available content for today
- **Total Generated:** How many pieces you've created total
- **Total Used:** How many pieces you've marked as used
- **Usage Rate:** Percentage of generated content you've actually used
- **By Type:** Breakdown of generations by content type

## Tips for Better Results

### 1. Be Specific About Deals
The more detailed your deal information (restrictions, category, location), the better the AI content will be.

### 2. Understand Your Audience
Lake County Local Deals targets residents looking for value. The AI content reflects this with:
- Local references ("Lake County")
- Value emphasis (savings amounts, percentages)
- Community-focused tone

### 3. Customize Subject Lines
Pick the subject line that:
- Best describes your offer
- Would make YOU click the email
- Matches your typical communication style

### 4. Use Platform-Specific Content
Don't just copy Facebook content to Instagram. Use the platform-specific version:
- Instagram content is more visual/hashtag-focused
- Twitter content is punchier
- Facebook content is longer and more conversational

### 5. A/B Test When Possible
If you can, test different generated content:
- Try multiple subject lines
- Test different ad headlines
- Compare platform approaches

## Troubleshooting

### "Daily limit reached"
You've used all your available content for today. Your limit resets at midnight.
- Check your subscription tier for your daily limit
- Upgrade to a higher tier for more daily content

### Content doesn't appear
- Refresh the page
- Check your browser console for errors
- Make sure you selected a deal before clicking generate

### Generated content seems generic
The AI bases content on your deal information. Adding more details helps:
- More specific descriptions
- Unique selling points
- Clear restriction information

### Can't copy content
Try these fixes:
- Use the Copy button instead of manual selection
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser
